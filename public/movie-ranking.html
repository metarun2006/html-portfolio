The best Movies According to Tarun

My top 3 movies of all-time

Gangs of Wasseypur

This movie was released in 2 parts. I really like movie because of the time landscape it covers over the different generations of mafia bosses. The location, the dialogues the background music, the story etc. everything goes hand in hand to make it a cinematic masterpiece.

Baahubali

Simply amazing stroytelling and music.

RRR

Great work of fiction portraying India's freedom struggle against the British rule. I love the way 2 different characters from different walks of life with conflicting priorities, come to know each other and then agaist odds collaborate to fight against tyranny of British.
